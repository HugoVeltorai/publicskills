# First 90 Days

A Claude skill that helps a new executive win their first 90 days, from before day one through the end of the first quarter. It diagnoses the specific transition, then builds a tailored 30-60-90 plan drawing on the established frameworks, rather than handing over a generic checklist.

## What it does

A new executive's first quarter is make-or-break. Roughly half of new executives fail or leave within eighteen months, and the cause is almost never competence. It is the transition itself: no agreed definition of what success looks like, unclear expectations, slow context-building, and the absence of a deliberate plan. This skill helps a new leader get alignment early on what good looks like, then execute a structured entry rather than improvising.

## How it works

It diagnoses before it plans, because a turnaround and a thriving business you are inheriting need opposite approaches. It establishes the STARS situation (startup, turnaround, accelerated growth, realignment, or sustaining success), whether the move is an internal promotion or an external hire, the state of the inherited team, and crucially whether success has actually been defined or is merely implied. Then it builds a phased plan, before day one, by 30, by 60, by 90, weighted to that situation and kept to a few things done well rather than an overwhelming checklist.

## What it draws on

The skill synthesises the major works on executive transition, Watkins' *The First 90 Days* and *Master Your Next Move*, Bradt's *The New Leader's 100-Day Action Plan*, Goldsmith's *What Got You Here Won't Get You There*, Horowitz's *The Hard Thing About Hard Things*, Lencioni's *The Five Dysfunctions of a Team*, and *CEO Excellence*, alongside current research on why transitions fail. It brings the right framework to the situation rather than dumping all of them: STARS and the five conversations for everyone, the team-trust pyramid when the team is the problem, the wartime lens for a turnaround, relationship re-engineering for an internal promotion, and the six-mindset view for a CEO transition.

## When to use it

Reach for it when starting, preparing for, or newly in a senior role or promotion. It triggers on requests like "help me build my first 90 day plan", "I just got promoted to lead my former peers", "I'm walking into a turnaround", or "how do I make an impact in my first quarter".

## Structure

- `SKILL.md` — the diagnose-first approach, the spine of every plan, and the traps to avoid
- `references/frameworks.md` — the full frameworks from the major works, plus what current research adds
- `references/ninety-day-plan.md` — the phased 30-60-90 structure to populate

## Installation

Package the folder as a `.skill` file and install it through the skills interface in your Claude client.
